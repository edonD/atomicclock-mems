## Process Notes

- **Layer 1 (CAVITY)**: Single-mask DRIE through ~525 µm Si wafer to 1000 µm etch depth using Bosch process. Mask: photoresist + SiO₂ hard mask.
- **Layer 2 & 3 (Pt traces)**: Pt/Ti sputter deposition (Ti 20 nm adhesion / Pt 200 nm), patterned by lift-off. Both layers share the same metallisation step; separated here for DRC clarity.
- **Layer 4 (BOND_RING)**: Defines the contact area for anodic bonding to Pyrex 7740 lid wafer at 400 °C, 800 V.
- **Layer 5 (DICING)**: Dicing street guide only — no etch. Saw width ≤ 100 µm.
- **Layer 6 (OPTICAL_WIN)**: Defines the clear aperture area on the glass lid (no metal deposition here).
