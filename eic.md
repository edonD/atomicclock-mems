# EIC Accelerator — Funding Application Notes
MEMS Chip-Scale Atomic Clock (CSAC) — Rb-87 CPT

## What is it
European Innovation Council Accelerator — the EU's flagship grant+equity instrument for deep-tech startups.
Designed for: high-risk, high-impact innovations that are too early for private investors alone.

## Amounts
- Grant component: up to **€2.5M** (non-dilutive, no equity given up)
- Equity component: **€0.5M – €10M** (EIC Fund takes a minority stake)
- Can apply for grant-only (no equity) if preferred

https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-accelerator_en

## Application process (3 steps)
1. **Step 1** — Short online form (company + idea pitch, ~1 hour to fill). Can submit anytime, AI-screened.
2. **Step 2** — Full written proposal (pitch deck + business plan + tech description). Deadline above.
3. **Step 3** — In-person jury interview in Brussels (by invitation after Step 2).

## What to emphasize in the application
- **Problem**: GPS-denied timing is a critical vulnerability for defense, telecoms, IoT
- **Solution**: 3×3 mm Rb-87 CPT MEMS die achieving 8.84×10⁻¹² Allan deviation — 28× better than Microsemi SA65
- **Market**: CSAC market $335M (2023) → $617M (2030), 9.1% CAGR
- **Deep tech credentials**: Full simulation stack (physics model → mask layout → DRC → wafer tiling → thermal PID → package design)
- **Manufacturing readiness**: GDS-II layout complete, DRC 9/9 PASS, 701 dice per 100mm wafer
- **Dual-use angle**: Defense (PNT) + civilian (5G telecom, IoT, scientific instruments)
